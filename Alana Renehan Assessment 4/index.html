The Pinnacle Event of The Year

Presented by Lifeline

Lifeline WA’s pinnacle event, the Black Diamond Gala Dinner celebrates its 12th year in 2022. This event has become a standout in the Western Australianphilanthropic social calendar.
At Lifeline WA, everything we do is dedicated to ensuring that when a person in despair and reaches out for help, there is a highly qualified and experienced person available to take the call, listen and help that person stay safe for another day.
By joining us at the 2022 Black Diamond Gala Dinner, you will be making a significant and highly visible contribution to the Western Australian community.

Luc Longley

Guest Speaker

Luc Longley AM, is an iconic Australian and basketball champion.
Luc's inherent curiosity and courage led him to blaze the trail for elite Australian basketballers becoming the first Australian to play in the NBA. He also represented Australia in three Olympic Teams including the 1988 Seoul Olympics which delivered Australia's best result for the Men's Team.
Luc joined the Chicago Bulls in 1994 and was a critical component in their famous three-peat, winning back to back championships from 1996-1998 and setting an NBA record with 72 wins and only 10 losses. Luc was coached and mentored by the legendary Phil Jackson and played alongside some of basketball's most iconic players including Michael Jordan and Dennis Rodman. The Team, and this incredible accomplishment, were the subjects of the 2020 Netflix & ESPN Documentary, The Last Dance.
Luc’s openness, honesty and passion for life is truly inspiring. His story about mental resilience and tenacity will leave a lasting impression on our Black Diamond audience.
